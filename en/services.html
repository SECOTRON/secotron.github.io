Services

Senior IT, three ways.

SECOTRON is an independent consultancy. You work directly with the senior expert doing the work — no account managers, no hand-offs to juniors. Below: what we deliver, how we engage, and exactly how support works.

🧭 Consultancy

A versatile all-rounder across the stack — solution architecture, cloud engineering (Azure) and security. Equally comfortable whiteboarding a target architecture and getting hands-on in the platform that delivers it.

Long-term partnership

Embedded in your team

The heart of SECOTRON: a one-to-three-year engagement where we join your organisation as a trusted expert. Deep context, real ownership, and a single accountable point of contact who learns your business and grows with it.

  • 1–3 year engagements
  • Single accountable expert
  • Deep business context
Fixed-scope

Projects & training

Productized engagements with a defined deliverable and a defined end. Ideal when you need a specific outcome without opening a long-term commitment.

  • Cloud Architecture Review
  • Azure Landing Zone setup
  • Security Assessment & Hardening
  • Migration Sprint
  • Architecture Health-check
How support works. Fixed-scope projects are delivered to an agreed specification and include a short bug-fix warranty (typically 30 days) on the delivered scope. Ongoing support, changes and maintenance are available as a separate, transparent retainer — so you only pay for aftercare you actually want, and nothing is left to assumption.

  • Infrastructure-as-Code: Terraform
  • Terragrunt
  • CI/CD & DevOps practices
  • Microsoft 365 & Azure
  • Security fundamentals
