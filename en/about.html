About

Independent. Senior. Accountable.

SECOTRON is an independent IT consultancy based in Zedelgem, Belgium. We pair broad, all-round expertise with years of hands-on experience across SMEs and enterprise environments — and we believe in clear communication, healthy ambition, and doing what we say we'll do.
